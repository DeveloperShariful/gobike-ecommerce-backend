About GoBike

Welcome to GOBIKE. We are an Australian-born brand founded in 2023 by two mates/dads. Our journey began due to our kids' enthusiasm for electric balance bikes and watching them have so much fun! This motivated us to design a product that would elevate their riding experience to the next level.

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages are GoBike bikes for?

Our bikes are designed for a wide range of ages, typically from 2 to 13 years old, depending on the model (12-inch, 16-inch, etc.). Please check the specific product page for age recommendations.

How long does the battery last?

On a full charge, the battery typically lasts for 60-90 minutes of continuous riding, depending on the terrain and rider's weight.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer fast and reliable shipping across Australia. Standard shipping usually takes 3-5 business days. Please visit our shipping policy page for more details.
